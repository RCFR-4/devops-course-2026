# Introduction to DevOps Culture

Instructor: Breno Felix de Sousa

This repository will be used to manage the activities and projects of the course "Introduction to DevOps Culture".

Students will investigate DevOps practices through literature review and analysis of tools used in industry.

## Course Workflow

Each group will work on a DevOps-related topic and deliver weekly progress.

Week 1 – Topic definition  
Week 2 – Research question  
Week 3 – Article search  
Week 4 – Article selection  
Week 5 – Article summary  
Week 6 – Evidence table  
Week 7 – DevOps practices  
Week 8 – DevOps tools  
Week 9 – DevOps pipeline  
Week 10 – Draft report  
Week 11 – Final report and presentation

## Final Project

Each group must produce a report including:

1. Introduction
2. Literature review
3. DevOps practices
4. DevOps tools
5. DevOps pipeline example
6. Discussion
7. Conclusion
